    • Welcome

    We are a young, diverse, and interdisciplinary team of scientists dedicated to developing deep learning methods for medical applications. Our goal is to extract valuable knowledge from clinical routine data and advance diagnostics and treatment in precision medicine.S

    We are based in Aachen within the Deparment of Diagnostic and Interventional Radiology of RWTH Aachen in close proximity to the Netherlands and Belgium.
